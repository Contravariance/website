ContraVariance Podcast

ContraVariance is a Podcast about a variety of topics surrounding Apple, Development and Tech in general. It was started in 2018 by Bas Broek and Benedikt Terhechte. Bas left ContraVariance in 2020 to join Apple.

The podcast covers a variety of topics, here's a subset of things that were discussed so far: Refactoring, Technical Writing, Hackintoshes, VIM, SwiftUI, iOS Conferences, Chocolate, iOS and many more

We also had multiple fantastic guests from the iOS community (and beyond!) on the pod. You can find us here:
